About Rozef Foundation & Rozef Academy

Rozef Foundation & Rozef Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering holistic development through education, health, women empowerment, and social welfare. We believe in creating a world where every individual has the resources and opportunities to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized and underserved communities through comprehensive educational programs and sustainable livelihood programs in education, health, livelihood, and social welfare.

Our Vision

A future where every person has the resources and opportunities to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society. To build an inclusive society where every person has access to education, nutrition, opportunity, and dignity.

Our Motto

Educate. Empower. Elevate.

Empowering Lives through Knowledge, Care, and Compassion.

Our Core Values

Integrity

A commitment to integrity, compassion, and empowerment in all our endeavors.

Compassion

Understanding and addressing the needs of marginalized communities with empathy.

Transparency

Open and honest communication about our programs, finances, and impact.

Sustainability

Creating long-term solutions that empower communities to be self-reliant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

Rozef Academy

What age can my child join the Preschool program? +
Our Preschool / School Preparation Program accepts children aged 3-5 years. We focus on play-based learning, phonics, and school readiness activities to prepare young learners for formal education.
Do you provide online classes? +
Currently, we primarily offer in-person classes at our three centers in Aligarh. However, we do provide online support for competitive exam preparation and certain skill development programs. Please contact us for specific program availability.
