December 1, 2024

Admission Open 2025 - Rozef Academy

Rozef Academy is now accepting applications for the 2025 academic session for all programs from Preschool to Class 12, including competitive exam preparation.

Programs Available:

  • Preschool / School Preparation (Ages 3-5)
  • Early School Support (Class 1-4)
  • Learning Support (Class 5-12)
  • Competitive Exam Preparation
  • Drop-Out Batch (NIOS)
  • Career & Job Support Program

How to Apply: Visit our center or contact us at +91 82735 62607

November 20, 2024

Project Roshni Launch

We are excited to announce the launch of Project Roshni, our new community learning initiative focused on youth leadership and social responsibility.

Key Features:

  • Leadership development workshops
  • Community service projects
  • Skill-building sessions
  • Mentorship programs

Join us in empowering the next generation of community leaders!

November 1, 2024

Women Empowerment Workshop Success

Our 3-day women empowerment workshop concluded successfully with participation from 50+ women from across Aligarh.

Workshop Highlights:

  • Financial literacy training
  • Micro-enterprise development
  • Self-help group formation
  • Skill development sessions

Participants received certificates and starter kits to begin their entrepreneurial journey.

October 15, 2024

Nutrition Camp in Aligarh

Rozef Foundation successfully organized a nutrition camp distributing nutritious meals to over 200 children and conducting health awareness sessions.

Camp Activities:

  • Distribution of nutritious meals
  • Health checkups for children
  • Nutrition awareness sessions for parents
  • Hygiene education

Thank you to all volunteers and donors who made this possible!

September 25, 2024

Annual Community Gathering

Our annual community gathering brought together students, parents, volunteers, and community members to celebrate our collective achievements.

The event featured cultural performances, award ceremonies, and interactive sessions highlighting the impact of our programs throughout the year.

September 10, 2024

New Centre Opening - Ram Nagar

Rozef Foundation expanded its reach with the opening of a new center in Ram Nagar, focusing on health, nutrition, and community welfare programs.

The center will serve as a hub for health camps, nutrition distribution, and community outreach activities.
