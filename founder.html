Our Founder

Leading with vision, dedication, and unwavering commitment to social change

Dr Mohd Asrarul Haque

Founder & Director

Visionary Leader & Social Reformer

Dr Mohd Asrarul Haque is the visionary founder of Rozef Foundation, dedicated to empowering marginalized communities through education, healthcare, and social welfare. With years of experience in community development and a deep commitment to social justice, Dr Haque has transformed the lives of thousands of individuals across Aligarh and beyond.

His leadership philosophy centers on the belief that education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty and creating sustainable change. Under his guidance, Rozef Foundation has grown from a small community initiative to a recognized organization making significant impact in education, women empowerment, and healthcare.

"Education is not just about learning facts, but about empowering individuals to transform their communities and create a better future for all."

- Dr Mohd Asrarul Haque

Our Dedicated Teacher

Committed to excellence in education and student development

Passionate Educator & Mentor

Shadab Khan is a dedicated educator at Rozef Foundation, bringing passion, expertise, and innovative teaching methods to our educational programs. With a deep understanding of student needs and learning challenges, Shadab has been instrumental in developing our curriculum and ensuring quality education for all students.

His commitment to student success goes beyond the classroom, as he actively mentors students, helps them overcome academic challenges, and inspires them to pursue their dreams. Shadab's teaching philosophy emphasizes personalized learning, critical thinking, and holistic development.

"Every student has unique potential. My role is to unlock that potential and guide them towards a brighter future."

- Shadab Khan
