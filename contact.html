About Rozef Foundation & Rozef Academy

Rozef Foundation & Rozef Academy is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering holistic development through education, health, women empowerment, and social welfare. We believe in creating a world where every individual has the resources and opportunities to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized and underserved communities through comprehensive educational programs and sustainable livelihood programs in education, health, livelihood, and social welfare.

Our Vision

A future where every person has the resources and opportunities to achieve their full potential and contribute positively to society. To build an inclusive society where every person has access to education, nutrition, opportunity, and dignity.

Our Motto

Educate. Empower. Elevate.

Empowering Lives through Knowledge, Care, and Compassion.

Our Core Values

Integrity

A commitment to integrity, compassion, and empowerment in all our endeavors.

Compassion

Understanding and addressing the needs of marginalized communities with empathy.

Transparency

Open and honest communication about our programs, finances, and impact.

Sustainability

Creating long-term solutions that empower communities to be self-reliant.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and services

Rozef Academy

What age can my child join the Preschool program? +
Our Preschool / School Preparation Program accepts children aged 3-5 years. We focus on play-based learning, phonics, and school readiness activities to prepare young learners for formal education.
Do you provide online classes? +
Currently, we primarily offer in-person classes at our three centers in Aligarh. However, we do provide online support for competitive exam preparation and certain skill development programs. Please contact us for specific program availability.
What is the fee structure for academy programs? +
Our fee structure varies by program and is designed to be affordable. We also offer scholarships for deserving students from underprivileged backgrounds. Please contact us at +91 82735 62607 or visit our center for detailed fee information.
Can adults enroll in the Drop-Out Batch program? +
Yes! Our Drop-Out Batch program is open to anyone who wishes to complete their Class 10 or Class 12 education through NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling), regardless of age. We offer flexible timings to accommodate working individuals.

Rozef Foundation

How can I donate to Rozef Foundation? +
You can donate through our website, via direct bank transfer, UPI, or by visiting our office. All donations are eligible for tax deductions under Section 80G. Visit our Donate page for more details.
How to volunteer with Rozef Foundation? +
We welcome volunteers in various areas including education, health, women empowerment, and administration. Fill out the volunteer application form on our Join Us page or contact us directly. We offer flexible volunteering options including weekday, weekend, and project-based opportunities.
Where are your centers located? +
We have three centers in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh:
1. Patwari Nagla Centre - Main educational center
2. Maulana Azad Nagar Centre - Women empowerment programs
3. Ram Nagar Centre - Health and nutrition programs
Visit our Contact page for detailed addresses and maps.
Is Rozef Foundation a registered NGO? +
Yes, Rozef Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. We maintain complete transparency in our operations and provide regular reports to donors. Visit our Transparency page for annual reports and financial summaries.
Can I sponsor a child's education? +
Yes! We offer child sponsorship programs where you can support a child's education for a year or longer. Sponsors receive regular updates on the child's progress. Contact us for more information about our sponsorship program.
How can my organization partner with Rozef? +
We welcome partnerships with corporations, NGOs, and educational institutions for CSR initiatives, joint programs, and resource sharing. Please visit our Partnership page or contact us directly to discuss collaboration opportunities.
Do you accept Zakat and Sadaqah donations? +
Yes, we accept Zakat and Sadaqah donations through our Rozef Welfare Fund. These contributions are used for supporting education, health, and welfare programs for underprivileged communities, ensuring transparent and effective distribution.
